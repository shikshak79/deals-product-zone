How it works
01

02
Deal Sprint (2 weeks)
Before we meet, I audit every surface you have: homepage, deck, outbound, LinkedIn, recorded calls. I arrive with the analysis done. Three 90-minute sessions. You make decisions, I do the work between them. You leave with a committed position, a core story, and a homepage preview.
03
Deal System (10-12 weeks)
Three more sessions. I build the full homepage, pitch deck, and deal operation. You rehearse, review, and approve. During implementation, 2-3 real prospects run through the Deal Room. You see it working on live deals before I hand over.
04
Coaching + Care (optional, ongoing)
If you want sharpening on live deals, I stay in the Deal Room for 8 weeks. After that, quarterly check-ins keep the system current as your market moves. Both are optional. The system works without me in it.
